Project: "Gengar Escape: Whisper of Forbidden Memories" (Game)

It is a mouse controlled game where the player have to avoid traps and enemies, shoot enemies and solve cryptography riddles and puzzles. The player will have a health bar, will collect healing potions along the way.
Goal: collect item within shortest time possible to stay on top of leaderboard

Game Concept

Traps (Room-specific): There will be 4 rooms with specific attributes: Moving lava tiles, weak tiles, wall spikes, and laser maze.

Tasks:
----Solve riddles and puzzles to open door locks.
----Collect an item "Gengarite" before moving on.
----Riddles are randomized so that in case of multiple playthroughs the game is not monotonous.

Kills enemies:
Four enemies- static and shooter, follower and shooter, moves in a fixed path, circular shooter and follower (final boss).

Timing and scoring:
Priority of time and gengarites will be based on weighted average making sure both are equally prioritized.
Time and gengarite count will be recorded for leaderboard.
