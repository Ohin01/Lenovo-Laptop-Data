Game: "Gengar Escape: Whisper of Forbidden Memories"

Game on avoiding traps and enemies,shooting enemies and solving cryptography riddles and puzzles.
Goal: collect item within shortest time possible to stay on top of leaderboard





 Game Concept

Level One: The Maze of Trials
 Overview:
----
----Maze area with 4 rooms full of traps and tasks.

----To reach Level Two, player must complete all rooms, solve a riddle, and collect a key.

----Collect Gengarite (item) along the way (counted in scoring)

----No backtracking to previous rooms once passed to collect gengarites again

 Traps (Room-specific):

• Room 1 – Moving Lava Tiles

Touching lava decreases health.

• Room 2 – Weak Tiles Puzzle

Step once ➤ tile cracks.

Step again ➤ tile collapses, Gengar dies.

• Room 3 – Wall Spikes

Spikes close from both sides in a fixed tempo.

Getting trapped ➤ death and restart from beginning.

• Room 4 – Laser Maze

Touching a laser ➤ instant death and restart.
laser goes on and off in a specific time period

 Tasks:

----Solve riddles and puzzles to open door locks.

----Collect gengarite before moving on.

----Find the key in Room 4 to unlock the door to Level Two.

----The trunk with the riddle and key.

---- The riddle opens a number lock. If solved, the key to Level Two is revealed.

---- Riddles are randomized and based on cryptography / CTF-style puzzles. There will be 15 to 20 puzzle randomized for the doors so that in case of multiple playthroughs the game is not monotonous.

 Level Two: The Haunted Castle
 Overview:

A 2D maze-like haunted area.

In every room there is a timer. If player does not get out of room within time, the room will collapse.

Fight enemies, reach the final boss.

Complete within time, or the castle collapses ➤ restart from beginning of Level Two.

 Enemies:

Four enemies: static and shooter, follower and shooter, moves in a fixed path, circular shooter and follower



 Collectible Items
• Health Bar

----Decreases when hit or touched by enemy attacks.

----If health reaches 0 ➤ restart from current level.

• Healing Potion: Rare spawn that restores health.

• Shadow Ball

----weapon to shoot

• Gengarite

----Collect as fast as possible for a higher leaderboard score.

----Randomly spawns.

----Stops spawning once enemies are defeated.


Timing and scoring:

In level one,
time for each room will be recorded, considered for leaderboard. 
In level two, there will a castle collapse countdown and time recording.
Player has to finish level 2 balancing between time and collecting gengarites to stay on top of leaderboard.
Priority of time and gengarites will be based on weighted average making sure both are equally prioritized.
Time and gengarite count will be recorded for leaderboard


Features:
1.Health Bar
2.Healing potion
3.shooting shadow ball, reloading shadow balls (auto)
4.Solving puzzle and riddles to open doors
5.Solving cryptography to advance to next level
6.control by mouse movement, shoot through click.
7. Static+shooter enemy, moving in fixed path enemy, follower and shooter enemy, final boss (follower+ shooter)
8. moving lava tiles trap, closing spike wall trap, weak tiles trap, laser beam trap
9. spawning gengarites, spawning healing potion
10.Leaderboard based on minimum time and maximum gengarite
